RPI MOBILE

An app that will help you with your day to day life as a student at RPI. Read below to learn more.

ABOUT

RPI mobile is an app made by members of Rensselaer's Center for Open-Source Software. The app is an effort to streamline a lot of the information provided by RPI. RPI Mobile is available for both iPhone and Android, but each version is unique.

Scroll through the website to explore the different parts of RPI Mobile in detail. Hover over the menu icon on the left to expand the quick navigation links to jump around the site. Find contact information at the bottom to send comments and suggestions to the app or website developers.

ATHLETICS

The athletics section contains all the men's and women's sports, and you can toggle between the two using the gender selector in the top right. After clicking on a team, you can view more specific details such as:

  • Roster
  • Schedule
  • News
  • Records

Hover over a list item to get more information.
